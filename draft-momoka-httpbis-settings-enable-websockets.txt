         Define SETTINGS_ENABLE_WEBSOCKETS for HTTP/2 & HTTP/3
         draft-momoka-httpbis-settings-enable-websockets-latest

Abstract

   It would be nice if the server sends a SETTINGS parameter indicating
   support for websockets over h2/h3.

Discussion Venues

   https://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/ietf-http-wg/.
